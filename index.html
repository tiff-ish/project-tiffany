Movie List

The Piano Teacher (La Pianiste) 2001 From Funny Games, Michael Haneke's twisted mind. The most gut-wrenching movie. Makes Black Swan look like a Disney movie. Neptune Frost 2021 An afrofuturist sci-fi film by Saul Willams. It also has a great soundtrack. Problemista 2023 Torre's masterpeice. Strong visual identity that's lightyears ahead of its time. Julio can do no wrong in my book. Primer 2004 Shane Carruth is an engineer turned filmmaker. A very clever story that's intentionally made to confuse your understanding of time travel. Go through the Reddit rabbit hole after you watch.

Literature

Range David Epstein Biased against the idea that we're all born with a certain set of skills. We're all capable of so much more. Cost of Living Martyna Majok Beautifully written play. Though I confess the ending f*cked me up. On Work Derek Thompson A remedy for the workaholic. Got this for free when I was invited to attend The Atlantic Festival. Short but potent read. Parable of the Sower Octavia Butler This sci-fi novel serves as a parable for the modern world. I'll always recommend this book.
